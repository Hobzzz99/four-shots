§ 01
Essence

Espresso is romance.
Pink is the love letter.

FourShots is the corner bar that pulls four ristrettos before it pulls one bad one. The brand lives at the intersection of the precise (extraction times, origin notes, ratios) and the playful (bubble-script lettering, lipstick pinks, plum drop-shadows that look like stickers on a worn fridge).

§ 07
Voice & Tone

Confident on craft.
Casual on everything else.

— Pillar 01
Specific, not solemn.

We say 18g to 38g in 26 seconds. We don't say "an exquisite extraction journey." Numbers are sexy. Adjectives are tired.

— Pillar 02
Sweet without sugar.

Pink is the welcome. The coffee is the work. We sound warm because we are, not because the brand book says to.

— Pillar 03
Short. Like the shot.

Headlines under six words. Captions under fifteen. If it takes a paragraph to say, it's the menu, not the marketing.

✓ Say
  • “Fourth one's on us.”
  • “Dialed in. Pulling now.”
  • “Roasted Tuesday. Drink it Friday.”
  • “Hot, short, honest.”
✗ Don't
  • “Artisanal craft coffee experience.”
  • “Awaken your senses.”
  • “A journey of flavor.”
  • “Premium specialty grade.”
